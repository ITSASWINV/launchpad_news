Welcome to the official newsletter of Launchpad Kerala 2024!

About

Launchpad Kerala 2024, One of the most prestigious events of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates. Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to propel advancement and innovation within Kerala's employment landscape by facilitating the seamless exchange of ideas and expertise. For more information, visit the official site: launchpadkerala.org.

Highlights

Innovative Assessment Approach

Launchpad Kerala 2024 features a rigorous technical challenge and online tests to assess both technical and non-technical skills. This ensures that only the most qualified candidates move forward to interviews, providing unmatched opportunities for candidates and companies alike.

Flexible Interview Locations

Shortlisted candidates can choose to attend interviews in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, simplifying the hiring process for companies and encouraging maximum participation from top-tier talent.

Diverse Talent Pool

Launchpad Kerala 2024 offers a broad spectrum of candidates from across Kerala, catering to a variety of skill sets and experience levels. This provides companies access to high-quality candidates for numerous roles.

Strategic Partnerships

The collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn elevates the impact of Launchpad Kerala, ensuring a premier job fair experience. This partnership guarantees a thorough candidate assessment process while providing companies with top talent and enhanced brand visibility.

Event Roadmap

Step 1: Participant Registration

Participants can register for Launchpad Kerala 2024, marking the first step towards this exciting event.

Step 2: Company Registration Close

Companies must complete their registration by this date to be part of the event.

Step 3: Challenge Start

The technical challenge begins, testing participants' skills and knowledge.

Step 4: Online Exam

Participants take an online exam to further assess their technical and non-technical abilities.

Step 5: Consolidated Rank List

A rank list is compiled based on the performance in the technical challenge and online exam.

Step 6: Option Selection

Participants select their preferred options based on their rankings and available opportunities.

Step 7: Confirmed Mail to Participants

Participants receive confirmation emails with details about their selections and next steps.

Step 8: Interviews

Interviews are conducted with shortlisted candidates in their chosen locations.
